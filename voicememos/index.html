this is a list of voice memos I've recorded since 2016. this list started with my first iphone, until then I must have had a nokia. there should be some recordings on there from before, but I hold no hopes for being able to reach that far down. the oldest memo here was recorded in winter, the afternoon of the 7th of January, 2016. it plays the sound of having just stopped playing the piano at my dad's house in Jelgava, Latvia.

the timestamp is captured here, but wish I could remember the place of them all. I recorded these for no particular reason other than to keep the encounters near and have the option to reanimate them. the memos can be played all at once, if that's what your ears desire. and remember – you can press the space bar or * if you're on mobile to stop it all.
